Full Fitness

A healthy lifestyle website to track diet and fitness

Track your personal fitness goals with the help of this website. After logging in, the site allows you to set a weekly weight loss (or gain) goal, and then computes daily quantities of key nutrients based on your profile information. It then allows you to log your daily food intake, and queries the USDA database to determine how close you are coming to your recommended goals.

This was a group project. I developed the food logging and tracking functionality as well as the authentication.

Railroad Word Guess

A Hangman-style game with a railroad theme!

Each of the dashes displayed represents a character in the railroad-related word that you are trying to guess. Press a key to guess a letter. If that letter is in the word, it will appear in its proper place. If not, it will be added to the list of incorrect letters at the bottom of the page. If you can guess the word before you run out of guesses, you win!

This homework assignment demonstrates the ability to perform basic manipulation of DOM objects with Javascript as well as basic event handling.

Crystal Collector

A number-guessing game to test your memory skills

In this game, the player is given a random total that he or she is trying to reach. Each of the crystals on the screen has hidden constant value. When the player clicks a crystal, the player's total is increased by the hidden value of that crystal. The goal is to reach the random total exactly. Players must remember the values of each crystal to do this successfully.

This homework assignment demonstrates the use of jQuery to manipulate objects in the DOM.
